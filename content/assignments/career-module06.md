## Overview

This module introduces professional networking as a research and relationship-building practice rather than a performance script.

## Focus

- Build or revise a LinkedIn profile.
- Learn strategies for making professional connections.
- Plan for an informational interview.
- Explore tools such as Career Launch where useful.

## Why It Matters

Students often hear that networking matters without being shown what that actually looks like. This module makes the process more concrete and less mystified.
