## Overview

This module helps students think critically about organizational culture, unwritten rules, and how they want to participate in professional environments.

## Focus

- Examine cultural capital and affinity bias.
- Identify unwritten rules in organizations.
- Discuss what “fit” often means in practice.
- Reflect on personal and professional boundaries.

## Why It Matters

Career advice often assumes students already know how institutions work. This module makes those assumptions visible and gives students language for navigating them more intentionally.
