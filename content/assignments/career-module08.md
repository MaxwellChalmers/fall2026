## Overview

This module brings the semester’s career-readiness strand together in a final synthesis presentation.

## Focus

- Integrate goals, strengths, stories, and professional direction.
- Prepare a clear and polished presentation.
- Reflect on what has changed over the semester.

## Why It Matters

Synthesis makes the career-readiness work visible. Rather than treating each module as isolated advice, this final presentation asks students to assemble a coherent professional narrative.
