## Overview

This module uses design thinking to help students imagine multiple possible futures rather than locking too quickly into a single plan.

## Focus

- Explore “Three Possible Lives” as a design-thinking exercise.
- Draft a SMART goal for this semester.
- Practice turning vague aspirations into goals that are specific, realistic, and measurable.

## Why It Matters

Students are often asked to make long-range career decisions with very little structure. This module helps them see career planning as iterative and gives them a practical way to scope a next step.
