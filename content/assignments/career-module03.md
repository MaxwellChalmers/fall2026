## Overview

This module connects career planning to material realities after graduation, including cost of living, compensation, and inequity in negotiation.

## Focus

- Work through a “life after college” reality check.
- Discuss salary expectations and budgeting pressures.
- Review salary negotiation guidance, including pay-equity considerations.

## Why It Matters

Career planning is not just about interest or identity. It is also about resources, constraints, and power. This module gives students a more grounded picture of what work decisions mean materially.
