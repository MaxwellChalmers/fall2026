## Overview

This module helps students identify what they have already done well and translate past experiences into language they can use in professional settings.

## Focus

- Reflect on favorite experiences from the last four years.
- Identify strengths and accomplishments embedded in those experiences.
- Review durable skills and compare them to three positions of interest.
- Find examples of skills they already have and skills they still need to build.

## Why It Matters

Students often have more evidence of readiness than they realize, but they do not always know how to articulate it. This module helps them turn lived experience into professional language.
