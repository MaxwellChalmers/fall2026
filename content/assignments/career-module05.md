## Overview

This module focuses on how students describe themselves, their experience, and their goals in interviews and other professional conversations.

## Focus

- Build short career stories from past experiences.
- Practice “Tell me about yourself.”
- Use structured interview-prep tools such as Big Interview.

## Why It Matters

Students need more than a resume. They also need a way to explain what they have done, what they learned, and why it matters to someone else.
