## Overview

This module introduces the career-readiness strand for the course. Students complete the career quiz setup, begin a values reflection, and learn what career support is already available to them.

## Focus

- Complete the required career quiz and account setup.
- Learn that seniors have access to career coaching.
- Begin a values exercise focused on work, purpose, and environment.

## Why It Matters

Students often make career decisions in terms of jobs or majors before they have named the values shaping those decisions. This module slows that process down and gives them a support pathway early.
